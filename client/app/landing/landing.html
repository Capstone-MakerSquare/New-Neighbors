How to use Turf

Turf is a web application to help you find your best next neighborhood. Let us help you explore your new surroundings. Here are just some of the features:

Map

The Map shows the location you searched and will display the general neighborhood highlighted by a circle when you click on the name of the neighborhood. This will allow you to see where the neighborhood is relative to where you are headed.

Order and Filter

Once you have a list of close by neighborhoods you can filter them based on rent prices, distance, and commute time. You are also able to order the search results based on these qualities.

Neighborhood Glance

When you click on a neighborhood you are then able to look at close by attactions and amenities such as parks and libraries, museums, schools and shopping.
